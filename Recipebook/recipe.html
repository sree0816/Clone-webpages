Preperation

To make coffee, boil water and add 1-2 teaspoons of coffee powder to a cup. Pour the hot water over the powder and stir well. You can then add sugar, milk, or other creamers to taste. Adjust the amount of coffee powder to suit your taste preferences, and enjoy your freshly brewed coffee
