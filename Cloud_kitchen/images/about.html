Our Story

A Culinary Journey Across India & Beyond

Born in a Cloud, Rooted in Flavour

CloudFeast started in a small, warm kitchen during monsoon nights in Kochi, where aromas of curry leaves filled the air. Our founders, travellers at heart and chefs by passion, wanted to create a space where authentic recipes meet the speed and convenience of modern life. Today, we’re more than just a cloud kitchen—we’re a virtual food haven bringing centuries of culinary heritage to your doorstep.

Every dish we create is a chapter of our story. We source our ingredients sustainably, cook with heart, and wrap every order with a promise of delight.
